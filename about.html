Ghost Ship

Many years ago a curse was put on this ship and the entire crew for their sins. Since that wretched night, they have been capturing people at sea and holding them captive with the hope that they might be the ones to help break the spell, but no one has even come close. After you are caught, can you release everyone from this dark spell, or will you become a part of the cursed crew like so many before you?

ARVI

You’ve received a letter from a close friend, who’s a detective in the middle of a major case. She’s investigating mysterious disappearances in a wooded area near you, and she needs your help.

Enter Sanctum if you dare, and you’ll face terrifying secrets and paranormal occurrences. Solve the case and unlock ancient puzzles as you fight to find out what happened to your friend – and get out with your lives (and your souls) intact!

Christmas VR
