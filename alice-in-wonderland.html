Alice in Wonderland Escape Room Overview

You’ve always dreamed of entering Alice’s Wonderland, haven’t you? Well, the time to live your dreams has finally come. Let go of your expectations as you dive into Wonderland and all it encompasses.

To your unfortunate surprise, you’re greeted with the news that Alice will not be joining you, as she is on trial for a crime that she clearly did not commit. It is up to you and your team to free Alice from this unjust trial, and keep her safe and sound in Wonderland. 

To get to Alice and protect her head from the Red Queen’s cruel and unfair sentence, you and your team must solve a series of clues and puzzles. Can you save her in time to free her from the guillotine, or will it be “off with her head!” by the Red Queen’s demands? Play the Alice in Wonderland Escape room and discover the outcome!
