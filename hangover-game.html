Mafia Escape Room Los Angeles Overview

Life in the Mafia is not easy. It’s full of suspense, challenge, and adversity. You and your friends were born into this life, and it’s been one wild ride.

The Boss has been in prison for nearly ten years, and tomorrow he finally gets released. It’s been nothing but chaos since he’s been gone; there’s been no order. You and the gang are ecstatic for his return, so the night before he arrives you go out and celebrate…

You may have celebrated too hard, because when you wake up with your hangover, neither of you remember what happened last night. To your utter terror, you all realize you hid the Boss’s cash and can’t remember where. You look at the time...

The Boss will arrive in an hour, and the first thing he’ll want to know is the whereabouts of his money. Can you retrace your steps and get to the cash in time, or will you face the wrath of the Boss on his first day back from prison? Play the Mafia escape room and find out.
