The Best Escape Rooms in LA

Established in 2015, 60out Escape Rooms presents a unique and interactive live-action adventure game experience. In our immersive stories, players like you and your friends, family, or coworkers become active participants with a front-row seat to the action. You must work as a team to solve intriguing problems, escape from precarious situations, and unravel the plot before time runs out!
Recently voted the best escape rooms in LA, we’re proud to offer 23 unique, thrilling, and captivating physical games. So if you’re a thrill-seeker in the Greater Los Angeles area, you’ve come to the right place. You’ll find some of the most exciting, engaging, and obstacle-driven escape rooms around.
Inspired by fan-favorite books, video games, and television shows, each room takes you on a high-action quest that is completely immersive and extraordinarily realistic. We let you pick your reality: come aboard the Titanic and test your ability to work under pressure as you attempt to save everyone on the ship; fulfill your goal to take down the Juarez Cartel as one of the leading DEA agents in America; dig through Grandma’s house in search for her large inheritance since she’s disappeared and left nothing but a small note. At 60out Escape Rooms, anything is possible — as long as you have what it takes to make it out in time.
It all started when we visited our first escape room in 2013, and very quickly became obsessed with the idea of a time-sensitive, fully-immersive game that can be tailored to any theme and story. Since then, we’ve worked tirelessly to create our own escape rooms that offer unique plotlines and innovative puzzles. And while you’d be hard-pressed to find such exciting escape rooms anywhere else, you can play our thrilling escape rooms at any one of our five Los Angeles locations.
We have an escape room for everyone, from adventurers to thrill-seekers to horror lovers. Are you ready to find your perfect escape room experience? It’s time to try 60out!
