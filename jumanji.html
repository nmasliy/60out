08 Nov 2017

For my 13th escape room, Grandma's Master Plan definitely ranks among my top few. The time limit for this room is 75 minutes as opposed to 60 due to how challenging it is. My group of 6 (who have all done escape rooms) finished with 3 minutes left to spare! Here are my highlights:

- Storyline: This room probably has the best storyline among all the escape rooms I have done. We (Grandchildren) have to find out what Grandma has been up to. I loved how we learned more and more about Grandma/plot as we progressed through the room.

- Theme: Similar to the storyline, the decor was on point. Not many red herrings either which was awesome!

- Puzzle Variety: Every puzzle was different and challenging. Most of the puzzles made sense too (I say most because there's always 1 or 2 puzzles that you let your teammates carry). There was cool technology involved with most of them - most to unlock more clues, which is expected.

- Other thoughts: This room seems kind of worn out. Yes, all the puzzles are fantastic, the room is great, but some things not vital to unlocking the next puzzle weren't working. A light/indicator didn't turn on, and when we were opening drawers, we accidentally pulled off the entire shelf...

I highly recommend this room. It is definitely fun and challenging so bring a good group of people who can think and work well together. Since it's on Melrose, there's metered street parking AND free lot parking in the alleyway behind the building.

Jumanji: Welcome To The Jungle Escape Room Overview

Step Into the Jungle!

It’s time you experience the most raved-about Escape Room in Los Angeles - Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle. Grab your binoculars and enter the world of Jumanji as you know it. Extending the narrative of the classic and it’s sequel, this escape room is filled with wild, untamed adventure that you and your friends and family will never forget. It’s time to leave the real world behind and play a game like you’ve never played before!

Based on the Original Stories

Jumanji is classic film that captivated the world and sucked all who watched into an imaginative world of the wild. 22 years later, this world re-appeared more wild and intense than ever before. This escape room encaptures the universe of both Jumanji and Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, bringing you in and not letting go. If you’ve ever wondered what it’s like to Play Jumanji, play this escape room and find out.

Top Notch Interactive Production & Gadgets

The time, brainpower, and technology put into this escape room is like nothing anyone’s seen before. Partnering up with Sony Pictures, we worked with master engineers, producers, and scriptwriters to bring you the real Jumanji experience. 

Teamwork & Character Role-play

The moment you are sucked into the world of Jumanji, you are placed into a special role that you must carry out in order to escape the room. You and each of your teammates will embody a unique character with your own special skills and abilities. Work together and use your strengths in harmony so that you can survive Jumanji. 

Your Adventure Begins Here

With your team of 4-8 players, you begin by entering the bedroom of one of your close friends. You’re looking for him, but it doesn’t seem like he’s in there. Like any good friends would, you take his absence as an opportunity to snoop around a little bit. Little do you know what you’re in for…

As you work your way through a series of clues and puzzles, you encounter the game of Jumanji. Choose your player, and get ready to be sucked into a universe of creatures, creepers, and the likes. Your only goal: make sure you and your friends survive Jumanji. Book the Jumanji escape room at 60OUT today!
